ARMAAN MALIK

Short Description of the Artist

Armaan Malik is a popular Indian singer and songwriter who is known for his soulful and melodious voice. He was born on July 22, 1995, in Mumbai, India, to a family with a musical background. Armaan started his career in music as a playback singer in Bollywood films, and later went on to release several hit singles and albums. He has won numerous awards for his work, including the Filmfare Award for Best Male Playback Singer in 2016, and has also been a judge on several reality singing shows. Armaan is considered to be one of the most talented and promising young artists in the Indian music industry today.
